रथी नगरचं विश्वासू किराणा दुकान

राम प्रोव्हिजन is a family-run grocery store in the heart of Rathi Nagar, Amravati. For neighbours here, it isn't just a shop — it's where the household's monthly list gets filled, where the festival shopping happens, and where quality is never compromised.

We stock premium, carefully chosen goods across every category — grains, dals, oils, spices, snacks and daily essentials — at fair prices. What keeps families coming back is simple: विश्वास (trust), a warm welcome, and goods we'd happily put on our own table.
